About the Lab

Addressing the critical sustainability paradox of accelerating digital transformation against its environmental footprint.

Our Vision

Introducing Green Computing research initiatives nationally and internationally.

Our Mission

Conducting cutting-edge research work in the field of Green Computing that includes green hardware and software-based system design and development using Cloud Computing, Smart IoT, Smart Grid, etc. Finally, the objective is to publish novel work in the top-tier venues, providing training for the students, and developing national policy through interdisciplinary research collaboration.

Core Objectives

Research Excellence

Pursue applied research in energy-efficient computing and publish in reputable, high-impact venues.

Student Training

Supervise MSc projects and offer comprehensive seminars to build deep expertise in sustainable IT.

Interdisciplinary

Partnering with engineering, environmental science, and medical fields on unified smart projects.

Industry Engagement

Working with telecom/energy companies and government agencies to pilot green ICT solutions nationally.
