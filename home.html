Our Strategy

Lab Development Roadmap

The GCI Lab executes a strategic two-phase implementation approach designed to maximize immediate research impact while securing long-term sustainability.

1

Phase 1: Virtual Lab Current Active Phase

Focusing on conceptual work, software development, cloud platforms (AWS, Google Cloud), open-source tools, and simulated environments to ensure rapid initiation.

2

Phase 2: Physical Infrastructure

Future expansion to a physical space equipped with ARM/GPU servers, IoT sensor rigs, and renewable energy demonstration hardware.
